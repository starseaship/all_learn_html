1. Contrast Paragraph 是什么

Contrast paragraph 用来说明两个事物之间的不同点。写作时必须明确两个比较对象，并使用相同的比较项目。
Chapter 5Chapter 6
Comparative paragraph：说明相同点Contrast paragraph：说明不同点
A and B differ in several ways.
A和B在几个方面不同。

比较跑步与步行时，可分别比较intensity、chance of becoming ill、weight loss。不能用A的价格去对比B的舒适度。

2. 段落结构

① Topic Sentence

主题句必须包含两个比较对象，并说明两者存在差异。

句型说明
A and B differ in several ways.differ是动词。
A and B are different.different是形容词。
B is different from A.固定搭配different from。
There are several differences between A and B.difference是名词。

② Supporting Sentences

正文通常写2—3个不同点，每个不同点后可加解释、事实、细节或例子。

The most significant difference is their intensity.
For example, people who run faster and longer may suffer from the common cold more often.

③ Concluding Sentence

总结主要差异，不加入新观点。

In conclusion, there are several important differences between A and B.

直接说明不同

结构例句与注意

A and B differ.不能写A and B are differ。
Unlike A, B ...Unlike British English, American English spells words more as they sound.

Signal格式

段落顺序

功能Signal
第一个重点First, / The first difference is ...

4. 比较级与词性

规则例子
短形容词加-erfast→faster; small→smaller; high→higher
辅音+y：y变i加-ereasy→easier; heavy→heavier; happy→happier
较长形容词用more / lessmore comfortable; less expensive; more convenient
At-home care is less expensive than residential care.
The rate is much higher than that in Australia.
词词性正确结构
differ动词A and B differ.
different形容词A and B are different.
difference名词There is a difference between A and B.
高频错误：more high ×；different with ×；are differ ×；比较级后漏than。

5. 整段改错检查顺序

  1. 主题句是否写出两个比较对象。
  2. 所有句子是否围绕主题，有无无关句。
  3. First、Second、Finally、For example等顺序是否合理。
  4. A和B是否比较同一个项目。
  5. 句子是否完整，有无两个完整句子只用逗号连接。
  6. 主谓一致、单复数、冠词、代词、时态是否正确。
  7. differ / different / difference与比较级是否正确。
  8. 连接词的句号、逗号与大小写是否正确。
  9. 结论是否只总结前文。

可能考试题型

选择Topic Sentence

选出包含两个对象且概括全段的主题句。

填写Signal Words

while、but、unlike、however、different from等。

补充一句话

根据上下文补支持句、例子或总结并加signal。

整段改错

语法、词性、标点、大小写和连接词混合。

删除无关句

删除没有参与A/B比较的句子。

比较级填空

-er、more、less、than、much。

词性辨析

differ / different / difference。

根据表格写段落

写主题句、2—3个不同点和结论。

7. 考前速记

必背句型
  • A and B differ in several ways.
  • B is different from A.
  • Unlike A, B ...
  • A ..., while B ...
  • A. However, B.
  • A is more/less ... than B.
最容易丢分
  • different from
  • differ / different / difference
  • Although不加but
  • 连接副词后逗号
  • 比较级后than
