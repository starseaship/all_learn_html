第二章：信息段落复习

Information Paragraphs · 支持句、Signal Words、相关性判断与逗号规则

核心概念支持句连接词逗号考试题型练习

1. Information Paragraph 是什么

Information paragraph 用不同方式提供关于某一主题的信息，通常由主题句、支持句和结论句组成。
  1. Topic sentence：说明主题与main idea。
  2. Supporting sentences：用理由、事实、细节或例子支持主题句。
  3. Concluding sentence：总结前文，不加入新观点。
Contact lenses have made the lives of nearsighted people more convenient in several simple, yet very important ways.
隐形眼镜通过几个简单却重要的方面，让近视者的生活更加方便。

范文支持点：更宽的视野；更方便进行运动；适合戴太阳镜的人。

2. Supporting Sentences 支持句

支持句必须直接支持主题句中的topic和main idea。只与大主题有关系，但没有支持main idea的句子，也应判为无关。

功能例子
ReasonContact lenses give a wider field of vision.
Fact / DetailThey make it easier for people to play sports.
ExampleFor example, turning off appliances can save electricity.
Topic sentence: There are many things that we can do to fight global warming.
句子判断原因
Cleaning the filter can make your air conditioner more efficient.○减少能源浪费。
You can make money if you recycle cans.×重点是赚钱，没有直接支持“应对全球变暖”。
You can save electricity if you do not leave appliances on standby.○直接支持节能。
Recycling at home can reduce carbon dioxide.○直接支持减少碳排放。
判断时先圈出topic和main idea，再逐句问：这句话如何支持main idea？回答不出来，通常就该删除。

3. Signal Words

功能常用表达标点
第一个重点First, / First of all,后加逗号
第二个重点Second, / Next,后加逗号
增加信息In addition, / Also,In addition后加逗号
最后一点Finally,后加逗号
总结In conclusion, / To sum up,后加逗号
First, contact lenses give a wider field of vision than glasses.
Next, they make it easier to play sports.
Finally, they are convenient for people who wear sunglasses.
Signal位于句首时，同时检查：首字母大写、signal后的逗号。

4. Punctuation: Commas

① Signal words后

First, decide how much you should study.
Finally, having an accurate memory is necessary.

② and / but / so连接两个完整分句时

The campus is beautiful, so many people visit it.
The library is large, and it contains many resources.
The system is useful, but it is expensive.

只有两边都能独立成为完整句子时，才在and / but / so前加逗号。

③ 从属从句位于句首时

Although Japanese is written in different directions, the same commas are used.
When Western literature was brought to Japan, commas were introduced.
If the conditions change, the result may be different.
Because the library is large, many students use it.
从属从句在后面时通常不加逗号：Many students use the library because it is large.

④ 列举三个或以上项目

The library has audiobooks, CDs, and DVDs.
Study intervals, collocation information, and recall speed are important.

⑤ including等补充说明

The library has many resources, including audiobooks, CDs, and DVDs.

5. 整段改错检查顺序

  1. 主题句是否清楚。
  2. 支持句是否真正支持main idea。
  3. 是否有无关句需要删除。
  4. First / Next / Finally等signal是否合适。
  5. signal后是否有逗号，句首是否大写。
  6. and / but / so前是否需要逗号。
  7. 句首从属从句后是否有逗号。
  8. 列举项目之间是否有逗号。
  9. 结论是否总结前文。

可能考试题型

判断支持句○或×

判断每句是否支持topic sentence。

删除无关句

删除不支持main idea的内容。

补写支持句

根据主题句补2—3个理由或事实。

填写Signal

选择First、Next、Finally等。

补逗号

检查signal、分句、从属从句与列举。

整段改错

混合相关性、大小写、标点与句子结构。

6. 模拟练习

练习1：判断支持句

Topic sentence: There are several ways to save electricity at home.

① Turn off lights when you leave a room.
② Electricity was discovered a long time ago.
③ Do not leave appliances on standby.

答案：①○　②×　③○
练习2：补Signal

_____ contact lenses give users a wider field of vision.

First, contact lenses give users a wider field of vision.
练习3：补逗号

Although the library is small it contains books CDs and DVDs.

Although the library is small, it contains books, CDs, and DVDs.
练习4：and前是否加逗号

The campus is beautiful and many visitors come every year.

The campus is beautiful, and many visitors come every year.
练习5：整段改错

Our school has several useful facilities. first the library has books computers and study rooms. Next students can use the gym after class. Finally the cafeteria is inexpensive so many students eat there.

Our school has several useful facilities. First, the library has books, computers, and study rooms. Next, students can use the gym after class. Finally, the cafeteria is inexpensive, so many students eat there.

7. 考前速记

支持句
  • 必须支持topic与main idea
  • 可提供reason、fact、detail、example
  • 无关句要删除
逗号四大类
  • signal后
  • and / but / so连接完整分句前
  • 句首从属从句后
  • 三个以上项目的列举中
