第三章：观点段落复习

Opinion Paragraphs · 观点表达、理由说明、结论句、because 与 so

核心概念段落结构结论句观点表达because / so改错清单考试题型模拟练习

1. Opinion Paragraph 是什么

Opinion paragraph 用来表达并解释作者对某个具体主题的看法。不能只写“我喜欢”或“我不同意”，还必须给出理由和解释。
I think that Dinosaur Park 2 is a terrible movie.
我认为《Dinosaur Park 2》是一部很糟糕的电影。
  • 在topic sentence中明确表达观点
  • 用2—3个supporting points说明理由
  • 为每个理由补充解释或例子
  • 写出与主题句一致的concluding sentence

2. 观点段落的基本结构

部分作用课本范例
Opinion / Topic sentence明确作者观点I think that Dinosaur Park 2 is a terrible movie.
Reason 1 + Explanation第一个理由及解释The concept is ridiculous. Building another dinosaur park is unbelievable.
Reason 2 + Explanation第二个理由及解释The plot is unbelievable. The heroes release a bigger dinosaur.
Reason 3 + Explanation第三个理由及解释The movie writing is bad. The characters do not behave like normal people.
Conclusion总结理由或换一种说法重申观点People should save their money instead of watching it.
Opinion → Reason 1 → Explanation → Reason 2 → Explanation → Reason 3 → Explanation → Conclusion

理由必须真正支持观点

Topic sentence: I believe that living in the countryside is better than living in the city.
Supporting point判断原因
We can rent larger rooms for less money.○支持生活更舒适。
The air is cleaner.○支持生活质量更高。
The countryside is quiet and peaceful.○支持生活更舒适。
Many countryside cities are famous for fruit.×与“更适合居住”联系不够直接。
People usually have to buy a car.×这是缺点，与作者观点方向相反。

3. Concluding Sentence 结论句

结论句位于段落最后，通常总结supporting points，或换一种表达方式重申topic sentence。

Signal用途
In summary,总结前文
In conclusion,正式结束段落
Therefore,根据前面理由得出结论
For these reasons,根据多个理由总结观点
Therefore, everyone should experience living on their own to become more mature.
  • 必须与topic sentence的立场一致
  • 不能加入前文没有出现的新理由
  • 不要逐字重复主题句
主题句说“我不同意智能手机减少交流”，结论却写“智能手机不是最好的交流方式”，两者并非同一个观点；在结论中新加“可以分享照片和视频”也属于新信息。

4. 表达观点的常用句型

表达例句
In my opinion, ...In my opinion, the three most important characteristics are responsibility, consideration, and helpfulness.
I think that ...I think that regular exercise is necessary.
I believe that ...I believe that parents make the best teachers.
I feel that ...I feel that the plot is unbelievable.
I hope that ...I hope that my future coworkers will be responsible and helpful.
I agree / disagree with the idea that ...I disagree with the idea that smartphones cause people to communicate less.
In my opinion 位于句首时，后面加逗号：In my opinion, school uniforms are useful.

普通顺序

First, ...

Second, ...

Finally, ...

强调重要性

In addition, ...

Most importantly, ...

For these reasons, ...

5. because 与 so

because：后面接原因

I am happy because today is a holiday.

结果 + because + 原因

so：前面是原因，后面是结果

Today is a holiday, so I am happy.

原因, so + 结果

原句顺序连接方法
My father is cooking dinner. My mother is not feeling well.My father is cooking dinner because my mother is not feeling well.
It is raining outside. Take an umbrella.It is raining outside, so take an umbrella.
Peter is nervous. This is his first public speech.Peter is nervous because this is his first public speech.
常见错误：把原因和结果接反；Because some people panic easily. 单独成句形成残句；用so连接两个完整分句时漏掉前面的逗号。
正确：Hiding the truth can sometimes be a good idea because some people panic easily.
正确：Some people panic easily, so hiding the truth can sometimes be a good idea.

6. 整段改错检查清单

  • topic sentence是否明确表达观点
  • 观点是否围绕具体主题
  • 每个理由是否支持观点
  • 理由后是否有解释或例子
  • 是否有与观点相反的支持句
  • First / Second / Finally后是否有逗号
  • In my opinion后是否有逗号
  • because后是否真的是原因
  • so前是否真的是原因
  • so连接完整分句时前面是否有逗号
  • Because从句是否错误地单独成句
  • 结论是否与主题句一致
  • 结论是否加入新信息
  • 结论是否使用合适signal

《Is It Better to Always Tell the Truth?》一类题要同时检查原因与结果的逻辑顺序、句子完整性以及标点。

7. 可能出现的考试题型

选择合适的结论句

判断是否与topic sentence一致，是否加入新内容。

选择Supporting Point

选择真正支持观点的理由。

补写Concluding Sentence

使用In summary、In conclusion或Therefore。

填写观点表达

使用In my opinion、I think that、I believe that等。

because / so填空

根据原因和结果的顺序选择。

合并两个句子

保持原语序，用because或so合并。

整段改错

检查残句、逗号、signal、逻辑和结论。

完成Opinion Outline

写topic sentence、三个理由、解释及结论。

8. 模拟练习

练习1：选择结论句

Topic sentence: I believe that school uniforms are useful for students.

A. In addition, uniforms are often blue or black.
B. In conclusion, school uniforms can make school life simpler and more equal.
C. Therefore, all students should buy expensive clothes.

答案：B。它重申观点，没有加入无关或相反内容。
练习2：选择because或so

Many students feel tired, ______ they should manage their time more carefully.

答案：so。原因在前，结果在后。
练习3：修改残句

Living alone can make people more independent. Because they must solve problems by themselves.

Living alone can make people more independent because they must solve problems by themselves.
练习4：判断支持句

Opinion: I think that cats make excellent pets.

① Cats are usually independent.
② Some cats have green eyes.
③ Cats do not need to be walked outside every day.
④ Cats can provide companionship.

① ○　② ×　③ ○　④ ○
练习5：整段小型改错

I believe homework is useful for students. first it helps students review lessons. Second students can practice difficult skills. Because practice improves memory. In addition, homework teaches responsibility. In conclusion homework has many colors.

I believe that homework is useful for students. First, it helps students review lessons. Second, students can practice difficult skills because practice improves memory. In addition, homework teaches responsibility. In conclusion, homework supports students’ learning in several important ways.

9. 考前速记

段落结构

Opinion → Reasons → Explanations → Conclusion

逻辑连接

结果 + because + 原因

原因, so + 结果

观点表达

In my opinion, / I think that / I believe that

结论信号

In summary, / In conclusion, / Therefore,

最容易丢分的地方：because从句单独成句、so前漏逗号、结论加入新理由、supporting point与观点方向不一致。
