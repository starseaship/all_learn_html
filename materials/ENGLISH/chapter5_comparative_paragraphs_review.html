第五章：比较段落复习

Comparative Paragraphs · 相似点、比较连接词、too / so、either / neither

核心概念段落结构比较连接词高级表达改错考试题型练习

1. Comparative Paragraph 是什么

Comparative paragraph 用来说明两个不同事物之间的相似点。写作时必须明确两个比较对象，并围绕同一个比较目的组织内容。
American football and rugby are actually quite similar in how they are played.
美式足球和橄榄球在比赛方式上其实十分相似。

范文比较的三个方面：gameplay、rules、strategy。

第五章写相似点；第六章写不同点。考试会把两章的signal混合考查。

2. 段落结构与比较目的

部分作用常用写法
Topic sentence写出两个对象和比较角度A and B are similar in several ways.
Similar points介绍2—3个相似点First, / Similarly, / Both A and B ...
Conclusion总结相似点In summary, there are several similarities between A and B.
所有supporting points都必须符合topic sentence中的controlling idea。比较对象相同，也可能因为比较目的不同而属于不同段落。
Being a student and being a company employee are both demanding jobs.
适合：都要早起、都不能错过deadline、都要做小组项目。
Both students and company employees make efforts to improve their performance.
适合：向同伴求助、保持动力、学习或参加培训。

3. Signal Words for Comparing

表达结构例句
likeLike A, B ...Like soccer, basketball requires physical stamina.
similar toB is similar to ACountermeasures in Chile are similar to those in Japan.
bothBoth A and B + 复数动词Both Japan and Chile are at risk of earthquakes.
neitherNeither A nor B ...Neither Sakina nor her mother knew how to change the tire.
similarlyA. Similarly, B.Japan has strict codes. Similarly, Chile has strict codes.
固定搭配：both A and B；neither A nor B。Similarly位于句首时后面加逗号。

4. too / so / either / neither

肯定相似

Mariko is allergic to pollen, and Miyuki is, too.
Mariko is allergic to pollen, and so is Miyuki.
American football involves physical contact, and rugby does, too.
American football involves physical contact, and so does rugby.
前句谓语so结构
be动词so + be + 主语
一般现在时实义动词so + do/does + 主语
一般过去时so + did + 主语
完成时so + have/has + 主语
情态动词so + 情态动词 + 主语
so结构必须倒装：○ so is Miyuki；× so Miyuki is。

否定相似

Tickets to Hawaii are not cheap, and tickets to Australia are not cheap, either.
Tickets to Hawaii are not cheap, and neither are tickets to Australia.
Christianity does not believe in reincarnation, and Islam does not, either.
Christianity does not believe in reincarnation, and neither does Islam.
too用于肯定句；either用于否定句句尾。neither结构同样需要倒装。

5. 综合改错清单

  • topic sentence是否写出两个对象和明确比较目的
  • supporting points是否全部为相似点
  • 是否混入强调不同点的句子
  • like后是否接名词；similar to是否漏to
  • both是否与and搭配；neither是否与nor搭配
  • similarly后是否有逗号
  • too与either是否混用
  • so与neither结构是否倒装
  • 助动词是否与前句的时态和谓语一致
  • 国家、人名等专有名词是否大写
  • people、countries等复数主语是否使用复数动词
  • 是否有无关句需要删除

《Quality of Life in the U.S. and Japan》

问题修改
标题和专有名词Quality of Life；United States；Japanese
单复数both countries；people are
补比较signalSimilarly, many Japanese people ...
neither倒装the U.S. does not have restrictions, and neither does Japan
删除无关句删除“日本平均寿命比美国长约五年”，因为它强调差异

6. 可能出现的考试题型

填写比较连接词

like、similar to、both、neither、similarly。

Supporting Sentence分类

按照不同controlling idea分组。

补写相似点

根据两个对象写comparison points。

too / so改写

选择正确助动词并注意倒装。

either / neither改写

处理否定相似表达。

整段改错

检查大小写、单复数、signal、助动词和逻辑。

删除无关句

删除不同点或不符合比较目的的句子。

写结论句或完整段落

总结两个对象在指定角度上的相似性。

7. 模拟练习

练习1：选择比较词

______ Japan, Chile has an earthquake early warning system.

答案：Like
练习2：both结构

______ Japan ______ Chile have strict building codes.

Both / and
练习3：so倒装

Maria can speak French. Ken can speak French.

Maria can speak French, and so can Ken.
练习4：either / neither

Tom does not like coffee. Jane does not like coffee.

Tom does not like coffee, and Jane does not, either.
或：Tom does not like coffee, and neither does Jane.
练习5：删除无关句

主题：online classes和face-to-face classes的相似点。句子“Online classes are usually taken at home.”是否保留？

删除。它说明不同的地点，不是相似点。
练习6：整段改错

japan and Korea are similar. Both Japan or Korea have convenient transportation. Japan has many convenience stores. Similarly Korea does too. Neither country are unfamiliar with earthquakes.

Japan and Korea are similar. Both Japan and Korea have convenient transportation. Japan has many convenience stores. Similarly, Korea does, too. Neither country is unfamiliar with earthquakes.

8. 考前速记

基础

Like A, B ...
B is similar to A.
Both A and B ...
Neither A nor B ...

倒装

and so + 助动词 + 主语
and neither + 助动词 + 主语

最容易丢分：both/and、neither/nor、so/neither倒装、too/either混用，以及把不同点写进比较段落。
